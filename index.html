IndiaMeme Community Rules

Please follow these rules to maintain a healthy environment in the community.

Rule 1: Be Nice and Civil

  • All content violating this rule will be removed.
  • Follow Reddit's rules regarding violence and do not post memes about suicide or serious crimes.
  • Do not engage in hate speech or promote harmful behavior.

Rule 2: Do Not Cause Disruption

  • Respect the community environment.
  • Do not spam, troll, or engage in vote manipulation.
  • Do not impersonate other users or make deceptive posts.

Rule 3: Uphold the Relevance and Quality of Content

  • Make memes relevant to India or Indian topics.
  • Ensure content quality is acceptable—no low-effort or clickbait posts.
  • Avoid reposts and ensure your content adds something new.

Rule 4: Prohibited Content

  • No personal information or intimate media without consent.
  • Do not post explicit or discomfiting media.
  • Do not promote illegal content or self-promote without permission.

Rule 5: Follow the Language Policy

  • Titles must be in English, but memes can be in any Indic language.
  • Providing translations when requested is encouraged.

Rule 6: Use Good Titles and Appropriate Flair

  • Ensure your title is relevant to the meme content.

Rule 7: Do Not Make Inflammatory Memes

  • Avoid memes that target gender, sexuality, religion, caste, ethnicity, etc.
